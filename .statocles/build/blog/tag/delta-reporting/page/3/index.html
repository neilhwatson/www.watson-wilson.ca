Predict the future with Delta Reporting and CFEngine

Tags: cfengine delta reporting

Posted on 2014-05-13 by nwatson

[caption id="attachment_908" align="alignright" width="320"]Trending promises repaired and host count.[/caption]

Until now Delta Reporting was only able to show you the outcome history of all your promises. Now it can show you the future. --- DR's new trending reports reveal how many promises will be kept, repaired, or not kept in the future. It will also show you how many hosts you'll have the in future. Combined, these reports show a clear picture of how well you are doing at keeping promises in the face of host population growth.

Features:

  • Tool tips reveal details of individual data points.

  • A matching data table is provided.

  • Statistical numbers are provided.
