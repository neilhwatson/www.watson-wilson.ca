---
title: GNU Screen cheatsheet
tags: shell, cli, cheatsheet
---

<table>
<tr>
	<td>Change to other region</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a tab`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Change to other screen</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a 0-9`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Detach</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a d`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Help</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a ?`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Kill screen</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a \`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Kill region</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a x`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>New screen</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a c`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Reattach</td>
	<td>`screen -r`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Serial connection</td>
	<td>`screen /dev/ttyUSB0 9600`</td>
</tr>
<tr>
	<td>Split</td>
	<td>`ctrl-a s`</td>
</tr>
</table>
