title: POSIX regular expression cheatsheet
tags: posix, regex, cheatsheet

Alphabetic  [:alpha:]
Alphanumeric   [:alnum:]
Control characters   [:cntrl:]
Hex numbers    [:xdigit:]
Lowercase   [:lower:]
Nonespace characters    [:graph:]
Numbers  [:digit:]
Printable   [:print:]
Punctuation    [:punct:]
Tab or space   [:blank:]
Uppercase   [:upper:]
White space    [:space:]
